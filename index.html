Origin Story

Born from BlueWallet.
Forked for BC2.

GenesisWallet is a community-driven open-source fork of BlueWallet, one of the most trusted Bitcoin wallets in the world. We preserved everything that makes BlueWallet excellent — its security model, its clean architecture, its commitment to self-custody — and adapted it for the BC2 network.

BC2 (BitcoinII) is Bitcoin's conservative sister chain, preserving Satoshi's original proof-of-work code with a fresh genesis block. GenesisWallet gives the BC2 community a native, mobile-first wallet built specifically for this chain.

Every element — from the gold-amber color palette to the network parameters — has been carefully adapted. This is not an official wallet — it's a community project, built by the people who use it.

Self-Custody First

Your keys, your coins. GenesisWallet never has access to your private keys or seed phrase. Full sovereignty by default.

Fully Open Source

Every line of code is public and auditable on GitHub. A community fork you can trust, review, and contribute to.

BC2 Native

Purpose-configured for the BitcoinII network — correct chain parameters, Electrum server integration, and BC2 address formats.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Inheriting BlueWallet's battle-tested feature set, adapted and focused for BC2.

HD Wallet Support

Hierarchical Deterministic wallets with BIP39/BIP44/BIP84 support. Generate unlimited addresses from a single seed.

SegWit Native

Full SegWit (Bech32) support for lower transaction fees and faster confirmations on the BC2 network.

QR Transactions

Send and receive BC2 with QR codes. Set custom amounts, add labels, and share payment requests instantly.

Multi-Wallet

Manage multiple wallets in a single app. Separate personal, mining, and merchant funds with ease.

Electrum Server

Connects to BC2 Electrum servers for lightweight, fast synchronization without running a full node.

Watch-Only Mode

Import xpub keys to monitor wallets without exposing private keys. Perfect for cold storage oversight.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GenesisWallet?
GenesisWallet is an open-source, community-driven fork of BlueWallet — one of the most trusted Bitcoin wallets in the world. It has been adapted specifically for the BC2 (BitcoinII) network while preserving BlueWallet's security model, clean architecture, and commitment to self-custody.
What is BC2 (BitcoinII)?
BC2 is Bitcoin's conservative sister chain — a proof-of-work cryptocurrency built on Satoshi Nakamoto's original Bitcoin code. It preserves the foundational principles of Bitcoin while addressing modern challenges, maintaining SHA-256 mining compatibility and the original difficulty adjustment algorithm.
Is GenesisWallet safe to use?
GenesisWallet inherits BlueWallet's robust security architecture. Your private keys never leave your device, it supports HD wallets with BIP39/BIP44/BIP84, and the entire codebase is open source under the MIT License — meaning anyone can audit the code. As with any wallet, always back up your seed phrase and store it securely.
Where can I buy or trade BC2?
BC2 is currently available on NonKYC (BC2/BTC and BC2/USDT pairs) and CoinEx (BC2/USDT pair). You can also mine BC2 using any SHA-256 compatible ASIC miner by connecting to a BC2 mining pool.
Can I mine BC2?
Yes! BC2 uses the SHA-256 proof-of-work algorithm, the same as Bitcoin. Any SHA-256 ASIC miner (such as the Antminer S19 series) can mine BC2 by pointing it to a BC2 mining pool. Mining payouts are received directly in your GenesisWallet.
How do I get started?
Download GenesisWallet from the GitHub releases page, create a new wallet, securely back up your 24-word seed phrase, and you're ready to receive BC2. You can get your first BC2 by mining, trading on an exchange, or receiving from another user.
Is GenesisWallet affiliated with BlueWallet?
No. GenesisWallet is an independent, community-driven fork. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or supported by the BlueWallet team. The fork is made possible by BlueWallet's open-source MIT License, and all original BlueWallet attributions are preserved.
